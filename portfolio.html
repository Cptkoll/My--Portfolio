Past Projects

  • Project 1: Disney Ride Concept

    Description of my process: In 2024, I used the Blue Sky brainstorming process to develop a new ride concept for Disney, a single sentence summing up the ride experience: Journey with Maui to rescue Moana from the Kakamora. As I continued to brainstorm ideas, other concepts joined in: "Omnimover Ride System", "Use pressurized air for Kakamore darts", "Ride in Maui's fishook" and more. After that, I used Post-its to order the story and help visualize the attraction. When done finilizing the basic plot, I created a storyboard to outline the ride experience from the guest's point of view. Then, I planned the layout of the ride, including the track design and key scenes. After finishing planning my attraction, I created a detailed presentation to showcase my concept to my peers and teachers. I spent months creating a VR simulation of the ride experience, allowing others to immerse themselves in my vision.

    Challenges and Solutions I found: During my project, I faced many different challenges, from facing technical difficulties with the VR, to creative roadblocks in the design process.
